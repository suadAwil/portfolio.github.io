EDA
supermarket data analysis

The data analyst project focuses on an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a supermarket chain. The objective of the project is to understand customer behavior, preferences and patterns by analyzing various data sets such as sales data, customer demographics, and purchase history. The project delves into the sales data of different product categories to identify trends and patterns. The ultimate goal is to provide insights that will help the supermarket to improve its customer service, increase sales, and optimize inventory management.

DATA VISUALIZATION
JORDAN VS LEBRON

The data analyst project focuses on the comparison of two of the greatest basketball players of all time, Michael Jordan and LeBron James. It delves into their career statistics, such as points per game, rebounds, and assists, and analyzes them to provide a clear picture of their performance. The project also compares their team's performance during the seasons they played, highlighting their influence on the court. Furthermore, it includes a qualitative analysis of their leadership styles and impact on the game of basketball, providing a more holistic view of their contributions to the sport. The project aims to provide an in-depth understanding of the performance and impact of both players, ultimately identifying which one has better performance..
